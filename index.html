ABOUT ME


I am Abdul Karim Zaidan. My passion for architectural drafting goes beyond technical lines and layouts it's about bringing spaces and ideas to life with purpose and detail. I don't just draw buildings I think about how people experience them, how form meets function, and how every line contributes to the bigger picture. My creative side spills into graphic design too, which helps me approach projects with both structure and style. I am always learning, always building whether it's refining a plan, exploring new tools, or turning a blank page into something that speaks. If there's one thing you should know about me, it's that I'm not just here to follow blueprints I'm here to shape my own path, one project at a time..


Why hire me ?


Choose me because I bring more than just skill I bring passion, discipline, and a real drive to grow. I'm committed to learning, detail-oriented, and always looking to improve my craft. Whether it's technical drafting or visual design, I approach every project with purpose and a mindset to deliver quality work and keep growing along the way.
